Digital Portfolio Project

This is the final project I completed for my Technical Writing course.
Throughout the semester, we created important employment documents for a real job posting we found on the internet.
The focus was to give us an opportunity to practice making employment documents and strengthening our technical writing skills. I was required to focus on different factors such as
accessability and presentation.

Skills I Learned

With this project, I learned why it is important to incorporate contrast and balance
in all projects and documents I create. Further, I learned how to make a resume and which content employers look for.
